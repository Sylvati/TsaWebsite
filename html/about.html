Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications are required to participate in the program?

You will need to complete the training course provided by Nancy Vermeulen, a trusted space travel instructor, at https://space-training.com/

Where does the money go when I purchase a SpaceTrip flight?

All profits go towards raising funding for future space missions for the betterment of mankind. We are partnered with The Office for Outer Space Affairs(?) to ensure funding is given to worthy causes.

Are SpaceTrip flights safe?

SpaceTrip has not had any major accidents since our very first launch. We are partnered with NASA to ensure the safety of all passengers.

Why do I have to submit an application?

There are many factors that go into when SpaceTrip flights can happen. Certain conditions may prevent the flight you want to take being possible. Other conditions may require you to ride with a different group or date than you originally intended. Additionally, payment must take place over wire transfer to ensure money is safely handled. All of these factors make applications a necessity for SpaceTrip flights to occur.

Where do launches take place?

Launches take place at NASA's Kennedy Space Center in Florida.

How do SpaceTrip flights land?

SpaceTrip flights use parachutes to safely land in the ocean. Then, SpaceTrip sends boats to retrieve the aircraft and passengers.
